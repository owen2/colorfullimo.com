About Colorful Limo

Hi there! My name is Owen Johnson. I'm a software developer guy from Iowa. I have many hobbies, one of which is making oddly epic techno.

Colorful Limo got its name by accident. I was trying out a list of names and someone misheard a name and said "Colorful Limo, that's an awesome name." I never came up with a better name.
